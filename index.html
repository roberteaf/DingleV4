How Deposits & Withdrawals Work

💰 How do I deposit?
Click the Deposit button on your dashboard. Enter the amount and your Minecraft username. Then add ohioohiorizz on Discord and send the agreed amount in-game. Your balance will be topped up once confirmed.
📤 How do I withdraw?
Click Withdraw on your dashboard. Your balance is deducted instantly. Then message ohioohiorizz on Discord with your Minecraft username and we'll send the items to you in-game.

Usually within a few hours. If it's been more than 24h, ping on Discord with your transaction details.
